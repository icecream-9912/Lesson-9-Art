What Is Art

Art, a visual object or experience consciously created through an expression of skill or imagination. The term art encompasses diverse media such as painting, sculpture, printmaking, drawing, decorative arts, photography, and installation.

Art is mainly used as a creative tool. Many people do art to either decorate, have some fun or to be creative. But art is not only used to be creative. It is also used as a jib. Many people all around the world are known for having art as their job.
